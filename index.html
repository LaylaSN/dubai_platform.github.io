Our Purpose

Why do we need this platform? What our platform is designed for?

Regarding the huge tourism activity is growing at Dubai (The west of east) we need an effective tool that make it possible for investors to make decisions with where, how, when to invest their moneys based on scientific analysis for the reliable data collected from official resources.

Questions We Will Answer

    1. What is the relationship between tourism activity and real estate prices in Dubai?

    2. What are the top investment areas in Dubai?

    3. When is the perfect time to buy a real estate in Dubai?

    4. What factors should you pay attention to when seeking to invest in Dubai real estate?

Research & Overview:

Consists of four analysis, with a total number of charts equal to 13

Key Findings:

The real estate market was studied and analyzed from multiple angles, relying on several factors:

  • Tourism is not the primary driver of real estate prices in the overall market, but it have a significant impact in specific geographic areas and with certain types of properties.
  • Investment decisions should not be based only on price or location and several indicators should be considered such as tourism growth, price stability, and price attractiveness.
  • The best investment opportunities, according to the results, are not necessarily in high price areaAreas should achieve a balance between the factors we mentioned previously.
  • Timing analysis indicates that purchasing during certain months has more advantages than others, and the return on investment also varies depending on the season when purchase is made.
  • Risk analysis revealed numerous risks influenced by several factors, including price volatility, liquidity, and the delayed impact of tourism activity. So purchasing in some areas holds higher risks than others.
